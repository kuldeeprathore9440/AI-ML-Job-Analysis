Developed an AI & ML Jobs Market Analysis Dashboard using Power BI to analyze global job market trends in the Artificial Intelligence industry.
The dashboard integrates datasets containing company information, job roles,
salary data, skills, and geographic locations to generate actionable insights. 
It visualizes key metrics such as salary distribution, hiring demand by experience level, 
skill popularity, remote work trends, and geographic job opportunities. 
The project demonstrates expertise in data modeling, business intelligence, 
and interactive data visualization to uncover insights about AI career opportunities and industry demand.
